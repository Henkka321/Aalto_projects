Application content history

The below section is dedicated to the exported data of the Application content history domain.
Application content history contain insights, messages, or other timely content that appears in the Today, Fitness, Sleep, or You tabs from Fitbit's app.

Files Included:
----------

AppContentHistory.csv

The data for application content history is exported as a CSV file with the following columns:

  id                            - Id of the content entry
  activation_date_time          - Date and time when the content entry was activated
  expiration_date_time          - Date and time when the content entry was expired
  creation_date_time            - Date and time when the content entry was created
  last_update_date_time         - Date and time when the content entry was last updated
  status                        - The status of the content entry
  content                       - The structered data representing the content entry
